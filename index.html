Welcome to InstaSaveGram project website

If you've ever been worried about loosing your immortalized unique moments captured via Instagram app, here is the solution just for you. Here comes the ultimate tool to backup all your valuable photos and videos you've ever posted on Instagram! Now you can quickly and easily perform a backup of your photo gallery and enjoy browsing it directly from your phone storage. What InstaSaveGram basically does, it allows you to download Instagram photos and videos to your device memory, allowing you to explore them with no network at anytime, anywhere. Simple but how useful.! :) Basic features: – Save any of the photos from your feed – Save any of the photos you liked – Save any of the popular photos of the moment – Search for photos with specific tags – Select the photos you want to download one by one – Easily choose the folder to save the photos – Simple and straight to the point interface – Save Instagram videos – Perform a full backup with a tap – Allows the creation of a zip file containing the saved photos for compact storage and transfer. – Send your zip file to Dropbox, Email, Bluetooth, etc with a tap – No ads! Application currently is in development process, please show your support and report via email any problem or your suggestion. Disclaimer!! Please do not use this application to save photos without the permission of the respective owners. Respect the rights of the Instagram users. InstaSaveGram uses the Instagram public API and relies on the Instagram servers. InstaSaveGram uses the OAuth 2.0 protocol to authenticate the user.
